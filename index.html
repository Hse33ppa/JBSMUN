About JBSMUN

Our Mission

JBSMUN is dedicated to fostering international understanding, diplomacy, and leadership among youth through simulation of the United Nations. Our conferences provide a platform for students to engage in critical thinking, public speaking, and negotiation.

150+ Delegates

Why Participate in JBSMUN?

Public Speaking

Develop confidence and eloquence in expressing your ideas in front of large audiences.

Diplomacy & Negotiation

Learn the art of compromise and building consensus among diverse viewpoints.

Global Awareness

Gain deep understanding of international relations and current global issues.
